Who We Are

Started with one idea:
No one should be left behind.

On September 23, 2020, Mr. Govind Shukla founded InAmigos Foundation in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh — a Section 8 non-profit with a simple but powerful belief: collective action can heal society's deepest wounds.

Today, we operate across 28 states, run 6 flagship projects, and have trained 30,000+ interns who carry our mission forward.

What We Do

Six projects.
One shared purpose.

Each initiative targets a root cause — together, they form a complete circle of care.

01
🍚
Relief · Food & Essentials

Project Seva

Seva means service — and we deliver it daily. Regular food and clothing distribution drives ensure no family, no homeless individual, sleeps hungry. From community kitchens to mobile food trucks, Seva is compassion in action.

Daily Food drives across India
02
📚
Education · Children

Project Bachpanshala

Bachpan means childhood. Shala means school. Together, they stand for something every child deserves. We create learning spaces for underprivileged children — providing study materials, mentorship, and a safe, joyful environment to grow.

28 States Active learning centres
03
🐾
Animal Welfare

Project Jeev

Jeev means life. Because compassion has no species barrier. We rescue, protect, and provide feeding support to stray and vulnerable animals. Every creature deserves dignity — and we're here to make sure they get it.

Rescue Street animal protection drives
04
👩‍💼
Empowerment · Women

Project Udaan

Udaan means flight. We equip women with vocational skills, financial literacy, and the confidence to build independent futures. Through structured workshops and mentorship networks, we help women not just survive — but soar.

Statewide Skill empowerment workshops
05
🌱
Environment · Sustainability

Project Prakriti

Prakriti means nature. Through tree plantation drives, river clean-ups, and environmental awareness campaigns, we protect the planet for the generations who'll inherit it. Thousands of trees planted — and counting.

1000s Trees planted across India
06
🎓
Skill Building · Youth

Project Vikas

Vikas means development. We prepare young people with professional and digital skills for the real world. Over 30,000 interns trained. 250+ have joined us permanently. This is how we grow together — by growing each other.

30K+ Interns trained · 250+ joined permanently
